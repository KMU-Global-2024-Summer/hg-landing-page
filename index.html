HarvestGuard: Revenue Prediction Model

Overview

HarvestGuard helps agricultural producers forecast price fluctuations and efficiently plan their production.

Problem Statement

Agricultural producers face challenges in adjusting production volumes due to unstable market conditions.

Features

  • Price Prediction
  • Resource Management
  • Market Adaptation
  • Cost Reduction

Target Customers

  • Small-scale Farms
  • Agricultural Distributors
  • Government and Corporate Support Agencies
